National Parks Analysis

Examining sightings in proximity to protected natural areas.

Overview

This section explores how often reports occur near national parks. For each park listed in our points of interest file, we count sightings within a modest latitude/longitude window. Counts should not be conflated with confirmed incidents—higher numbers may reflect tourism patterns or observational bias. Our method is local-only: we do not query external services and derive all relationships from the provided datasets.

Activity Near Parks

Bars show the relative number of reports recorded within ~2° latitude/longitude of each park. Interpret with caution: the underlying dataset is synthetic and distances approximate.
