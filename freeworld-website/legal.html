Legal & Privacy

Our commitment to transparency and compliance.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

FreeWorld XenoData does not collect or process any personal data. All processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not transmit data to any server or third party. Therefore the site is compliant with GDPR and other privacy regimes by design. Users may export filtered results locally; these files remain within your control.

Local Storage & Cookies

The application stores preferences (such as high contrast mode and saved views) in your browser’s local storage. This data never leaves your device. You may clear it at any time via your browser settings. We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics.

Disclaimers

The information presented on this site is for research purposes only. While we strive for accuracy, the datasets are limited and may contain errors or omissions. Analytical results should be interpreted with caution and do not constitute proof of extraterrestrial visitation. We are not responsible for any actions taken based on the content herein.
