Weekly Anomaly Pulse

Track the last twelve weeks of unusual spikes in reporting activity. Each bar represents a z‑score relative to historical norms. High peaks warrant closer scrutiny, though outliers could reflect short‑term media effects rather than true anomalies.

Emerging Cities

Discover locations where sightings are first recorded in our data. New cities are highlighted along with the date of their inaugural report. These vanguard points help researchers identify expanding interest or novel flight corridors.

Dominant Shapes

Which craft shapes dominate in recent months? See the distribution of discs, spheres, triangles and other forms over the past 90 days. Variations can hint at shifts in technology or reporting bias.

Confidence Pulse

Follow the mean and median confidence levels in recent reports. Rising trends may reflect improved witness quality or instrumentation; downward shifts could indicate increasing ambiguity. Interpret cautiously as confidence is self‑reported.

Sightings Distribution

This compact scatter map visualises the geographic spread of recent sightings. Each glowing point represents a report within our local database. Regions with higher density invite deeper exploration in the Map section.
