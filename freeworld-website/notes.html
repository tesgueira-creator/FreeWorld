Research Notes

A living notebook of hypotheses, caveats, and alternative explanations.

Atmospheric Plasma Hypothesis

Could many luminous sphere sightings be ball lightning or transient luminous events? Several reports coincide with thunderstorms. More data on local weather conditions is needed.

Data Quality Caveat

Descriptions vary widely in length and clarity. Short accounts often lack detail for reliable classification, skewing shape distributions. Future preprocessing could weight reports by description richness.

Follow‑up: Triangular Waves

A clustering of triangular craft in early 2017 warrants targeted investigation. Are these correlated with training exercises or novel aviation prototypes?

Silent Gaps

Periods without reports may reveal observational biases rather than true absence. Holiday seasons and major news events correlate with dips in submissions.

Alternative Explanations

For each case we examine, we document mundane explanations alongside exotic theories. This balanced approach ensures robust science and invites sceptical scrutiny.

Methodological Note

All analyses are conducted locally. No third‑party services or trackers are involved. Users retain full control of their data and can save views for offline reports.
