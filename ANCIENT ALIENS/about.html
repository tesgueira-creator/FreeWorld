About & Contact

Editorial Policy

XenoOrigin takes a pro‑Ancient Astronaut stance while maintaining rigorous standards of evidence. Each page labels material as real or AI, hypothesis or evidence, and provides a reliability score. We prioritise transparency by citing our sources and presenting critiques alongside pro‑theory interpretations. Our goal is to foster informed discussion rather than sensationalism.

Team

XenoOrigin is produced by a small collective of researchers, coders and editors who share an interest in ancient mysteries and open inquiry. Our backgrounds span archaeology, physics, design and journalism. We operate anonymously to keep the focus on the content.
